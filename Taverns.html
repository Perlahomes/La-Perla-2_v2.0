Greek Traditional Cuisine

Laterna (Greek – Traditional cuisine)

Laterna is a Greek tavern celebrating the tradition of sharing good food and good company. We serve authentic Greek meze made with seasonal, locally sourced ingredients from classic dips and cheeses to grilled seafood and regional specialties. With a warm, welcoming atmosphere and a curated selection of wines, ouzo, and tsipouro, Laterna offers a true taste of Greek hospitality and a comforting, soulful dining experience.

Kardamo

The Kardamo restaurant in Kalamata is one of the most recognizable dining spots in the city. At 21 Sidirodromikou Stathmou Street, you will find a youthful and modern space adorned with wood and stone that will impress you, while the restaurant's jazz music selections will take you on a journey.

TripAdvisor | Google Maps

To Xagiati

At the Kalamata marina, you’ll find one of the city’s most timeless spots for culinary enjoyment: Chagiati. With a history spanning over 30 years, this traditional tavern is a staple for locals as well as visitors seeking authentic flavors and hospitality with character.

TripAdvisor | Google Maps

Leonidas Butcher Tavern

The dishes, ranging from lamb chops to sausages and offal, are prepared with care and expertise. The warm, welcoming atmosphere of the tavern combines with authentic Greek hospitality, making it ideal for family and friendly gatherings. The restaurant’s commitment to customer satisfaction is matched by consistently high-quality options in the realm of meat dishes.
